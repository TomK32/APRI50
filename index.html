Autonomous Planetary Research Individual 50 (APRI50) is a terraforming game set on a nearby exoplanet. You are playing Angelica Smith, highly trained scientist with a few biomechanic modifications, who was sent here as part of humanityâ€™s search for new planets to colonize.

After your arrival in orbit in a hyperspace ship you have successfully landed on the planet. In a barren landscape shaped by regular and unhealty rainfall you need to establish a base to start exploring and terraforming.

The terraforming comes with a twist: Machines with a biological intelligence are used for the processes and to make it more tricky the machines do have their own unique DNA, controlling their actions and features.
